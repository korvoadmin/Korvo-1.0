Why we exist

Good work deserves an opportunity to be seen.

Project A is designed for independent professionals, small businesses and customers who want a simpler way to connect.

Our Mission

We believe talented local professionals should not need expensive websites, large advertising budgets or complicated technology just to compete for customers.

Project A aims to give professionals a place to showcase their skills, experience, services, prices and completed work.

Customers should be able to compare local professionals, understand what they offer and request help without unnecessary confusion.

Local skills should create local opportunities.

Project A is being built to help communities support the professionals who live and work around them.

It begins in Atlanta, but the long-term goal is to make local services easier to find across the country.

Personal message

A Letter From the Founder

Hi,

My name is Chris, and I started Project A because I saw how difficult it can be for talented local professionals to grow their businesses.

Many people have valuable skills and years of experience, but they may not have the time, money or technical knowledge to build a polished website or advertise like a large company.

I believe hardworking people should have a place where their skills, completed work and reputation can speak for them.

Project A is being built to help customers discover local professionals and to give those professionals better tools to present their businesses.

This project is still in its early stages. The current website is a prototype, and there is a lot left to learn, test and build. I want to grow it honestly, one useful feature and one real conversation at a time.

Thank you for taking the time to learn about Project A.

Chris Contreras Founder, Project A
