WHY WE EXIST

Helping Good People Find One Another.

Technology should make finding work easier—not harder.

Every day thousands of talented contractors, cleaners, installers, landscapers, painters, movers, electricians, photographers and skilled professionals wake up ready to work.
Many spend almost as much time searching for work as they do actually completing jobs.
Meanwhile, homeowners often spend hours trying to determine who they can trust with their home.
Korvo exists to connect those two people through trust, transparency, opportunity and community.

FOR LOCAL CONTRACTORS

Our Goal Is To Help Local Professionals Grow.

Korvo isn't being built just to create another marketplace. We're building a platform that gives hardworking people more opportunities.

Whether you're just getting started or you've been in business for years, we want Korvo to become a place where your reputation, experience and hard work help you earn more opportunities.

We want Korvo to help professionals...

  • Find consistent local work
  • Build trusted reputations
  • Showcase experience and skills
  • Grow their business
  • Earn trust through verification
  • Spend less time searching and more time working

FOR CUSTOMERS

Hiring someone should never feel like taking a gamble.

Whether you're hiring someone to clean your home, install custom drapery, pressure wash your driveway, assemble furniture, or complete a major renovation, you deserve confidence before they ever arrive.

That's why Korvo is being built around transparency. We want customers to see real experience, trusted reviews, verification badges, and detailed professional profiles before making a decision.

We believe trust should be earned before the first message is ever sent.

Customers deserve...

  • Verified professional profiles
  • Honest customer reviews
  • Clear experience and qualifications
  • Safe communication
  • A simple hiring experience
  • Confidence in every decision
A MESSAGE FROM THE FOUNDER

Why I Started Korvo

Hello,

My name is Chris, and I created Korvo because I believe hardworking people deserve opportunities.

Throughout my life I've met talented contractors, cleaners, installers, landscapers, painters, photographers, movers and many other skilled professionals who genuinely love what they do.

The problem isn't their talent. The problem is finding consistent opportunities to showcase it.

I've also watched homeowners spend hours trying to find someone they can trust inside their home. I kept asking myself... Why should finding work—or finding someone to hire—be this difficult?

Korvo isn't being built to become just another app. My goal is to build something that genuinely improves people's lives.

I hope Korvo becomes a place where small businesses grow, reputations matter, honesty is rewarded, and trust always comes before transactions.

We're still at the beginning of this journey, but our mission will always stay the same.

Help local professionals succeed. Help customers hire with confidence. Build something our communities are proud of.

— Chris
Founder of Korvo
